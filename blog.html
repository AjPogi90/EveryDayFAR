Arroceros Forest Park

I went to Arroceros Forest Park in Manila, for a quiet escape from the city's noise. The park, a small urban oasis full of trees and plants, offered a relaxing stroll with lots of shade and mild winds. It was an inviting spot to ponder and unwind, emphasizing the value of green spaces in cities. If you need some time away from city life, Arroceros Forest Park is an excellent place for you to disconnect and enjoy nature.

Chuan Kee, Binondo, Manila

I went to Chuan Kee in Binondo, Manila, with some friends, and it was a wonderful time. The restaurant's lively atmosphere set the stage for a delightful meal. The signature rice was carefully seasoned. Chuan Kee serves traditional Chinese cuisine in a local setting. It's a must-visit if you're in Binondo and want some superb Chinese food.

Dangwa Flower Market

I visited Dangwa Flower Market in Manila, and it was a vibrant experience. The market was packed with beautiful flowers of all kinds. Although it was a bit overwhelming, I enjoyed exploring the small stands and how they create bouquets.
